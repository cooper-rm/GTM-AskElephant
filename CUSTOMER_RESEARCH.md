## Pattern Analysis

### By Company Size
- **Micro (1-20 employees):** Revvy, alldemand, Trailway Growth, Linq Ventures, hapily, Sequel.io — 6 companies
- **Small (20-100 employees):** Vendilli, Pest Share, Kixie, Prophetic Software, BoomCloud, Disruptive, REDO, Renew Biotech — 8 companies
- **Mid-Market (100-500 employees):** Finally, Copper, KLAS Research, Peddle, ITS, SchoolAI — 6 companies
- **Enterprise (500+):** Stryker (43K), Applause (1-5K), Indeed (15K+) — 3 companies

### The Real ICP (what the data says)
**The overwhelming majority are 5-200 person companies.**
- 14 out of 24 companies are under 100 employees
- 20 out of 24 are under 500 employees
- Only 3 are true enterprise (Stryker, Applause, Indeed) — and those might be
  single-team deployments, not org-wide

### By Buyer Title
- **Sales leadership:** Head of Sales, VP of Sales, Director of BD, CRO — most common
- **CS leadership:** VP of CS, Director of CS, CSM — second most common
- **RevOps:** RevOps, Head of Sales Strategy — emerging
- **Founders/C-suite:** Founder, CEO, Fractional CFO — small companies where leadership buys directly

### By Industry
No single vertical dominates. Mix of:
- SaaS/Tech (Kixie, Sequel.io, Copper, SchoolAI, REDO)
- Services/Agencies (Vendilli, alldemand, Revvy, Trailway Growth, Disruptive)
- Healthcare-adjacent (KLAS Research, Renew Biotech, BoomCloud)
- Other (Pest Share, Finally, Peddle, Stryker, Indeed)

**AskElephant is horizontal, not vertical.** They sell to anyone with a revenue team.

## Additional Customers Found (Beyond Website Screenshots)

### Detailed Case Studies (from askelephant.ai/customers/case-studies)

#### Copper — [case study link](https://www.askelephant.ai/customers/case-studies/copper)
- **Person:** Perri Langenbahn, Head of Customer Success
- **Company:** Copper (CRM Software, San Francisco, 100-200 employees)
- **Challenge:** CS leaders couldn't review growing volume of customer calls manually. Couldn't identify patterns, recurring risks, or coaching gaps across team.
- **Solution:** AskElephant enabled querying 1,000+ conversations simultaneously, surfacing aggregate trends and risks.
- **Results:** 1,000+ calls queried instantly, 5x faster pattern detection, 3x improvement in risk identification
- **Quote:** "It solved an immediate problem where I can now query thousands of phone calls and find aggregate trends with the links to calls that reference them."
- **Impact:** Shifted from reactive to proactive performance management. Coaching became consistent, data-driven, repeatable.

#### PestShare — [case study link](https://www.askelephant.ai/customers/case-studies/pestshare)
- **Person:** Scott Stollwerk, Chief Sales Officer
- **Company:** PestShare (Pest Control SaaS, 50-200 employees)
- **Challenge:** CS team spent 30-50 hours/month manually reviewing sales call recordings to extract onboarding details and re-enter into CRM. Bottleneck in sales-to-CS handoff.
- **Solution:** AskElephant automated call review, capturing conversation details in real time and writing structured data directly into CRM.
- **Results:** 50+ hours saved monthly, 100% call details captured, 6 days CS workload removed monthly
- **Quote:** "My team spent 50 hours a month watching sales recordings to manually prep for onboardings. But now AskElephant automatically adds that information to the CRM, saving CS days of effort per month."
- **Impact:** Faster onboarding, seamless handoffs, ability to scale without additional headcount.

#### Kixie — [case study link](https://www.askelephant.ai/customers/case-studies/kixie)
- **Person:** David Gable, Head of Sales
- **Company:** Kixie (Sales Engagement Platform, Santa Monica, 150-200 employees)
- **Challenge:** Sales reps lacked real-time visibility into prospect engagement. Missed critical moments when buyers experienced friction. Signals went undetected until deals stalled.
- **Solution:** AskElephant integrated into sales workflows, monitoring buyer touchpoints and generating real-time alerts when prospects hit friction.
- **Results:** 3x higher deal recovery rate, 80% fewer CRM errors, 90% faster intervention speed
- **Quote:** "One of my reps got an alert that said one of your deals is having trouble opening the pricing page. That second, they got them on the phone, found out what the problem was, clarified pricing, and moved them forward."
- **Impact:** Immediate rep intervention on blockers, improved pipeline predictability, automated CRM data population.

#### Vendilli — [case study link](https://www.askelephant.ai/customers/case-studies/vendilli)
- **Person:** John Caruso, Director of Business Development
- **Company:** Vendilli Digital Group (HubSpot Agency, Pittsburgh, 25-50 employees)
- **Challenge:** Sales team spent time after each call manually writing notes, updating HubSpot, and searching past conversations for follow-up prep. 20-30 minutes per call for prep.
- **Solution:** AskElephant auto-captured calls, created structured summaries, extracted next steps, and updated HubSpot immediately. Made past conversations searchable.
- **Results:** 100% calls indexed, 4x faster call prep (20-30 min → minutes), 70% more consistent data
- **Quote:** "Our reps reclaim time from administrative tasks and reallocate that time to improving our sales process or client meetings. AskElephant has become as essential to our sales process as our CRM itself."
- **Impact:** Cleaner HubSpot records, standardized sales process, more time for client interaction.
